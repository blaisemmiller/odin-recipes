Falafel Bowl

Description

This quick lunch is light, yet filling.

Ingredients

  • 3 falafel bites (20g each)
  • 1⁄4 cup (1 oz) pre-cooked quinoa cooked according to packet directions
  • 1⁄2 small (2 oz) wholewheat pita bread or pocket (or gluten free) cut into triangles
  • 1 tbs hummus

Salad

  • 1⁄4 lemon juiced
  • 1⁄2 tsp olive oil
  • 1⁄2 tsp chilli flakes
  • salt & pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup (1 oz) arugula
  • 1 medium tomato diced
  • 1⁄4 red onion thinly sliced
  • 1 sprig of parsley chopped

Steps

  1. Cook falafel according to packet directions and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl whisk lemon juice, oil, chilli, salt and pepper together. Add remaining salad ingredients and toss.
  3. Divide salad and quinoa between bowls and top with falafels, pita and hummus to serve.
